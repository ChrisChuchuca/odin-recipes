Chankonabe

Description

Chankonabe (ちゃんこ鍋) is a Japanese stew (a type of nabemono or one-pot dish) commonly eaten in vast quantity by sumo wrestlers as part of a weight-gain diet.

Ingredients

  • Tsukune
  • Pork belly - thinly sliced
  • Chicken thigh fillets - cut to large bite-size pieces
  • Chineses cabbage
  • Bok choy
  • Carrot - thinly sliced into rectangles
  • Shallot/scallions - cut 2" long
  • Shiitake mushrooms - stem removed
  • Enoki Mushrooms - bottom of stem removed
  • Kamaboko - thinly sliced
